## üé¨ What is Genova IPTV?

**Genova IPTV** is a next-generation IPTV player designed for everyone. Enjoy your favorite TV channels, shows, and VOD content on any device, with a beautiful and intuitive interface. Genova is free to use, with optional premium features for power users.

---

## üåü Key Features

- **Universal Compatibility**: Works with Xtream Codes, M3U/M3U8 playlists, and public IPTV sources.
- **All Platforms**: Android, iOS, tvOS, macOS, Windows, Linux, Android TV.
- **Modern UI**: Clean, ergonomic, and responsive interface inspired by Material Design 3.
- **Live TV & EPG**: Browse live channels with integrated Electronic Program Guide (EPG).
- **Favorites & Playlists**: Organize your favorite channels and create custom playlists.
- **Cloud Sync**: Keep your favorites and settings synchronized across all your devices (premium).
- **Advanced Playback**: Picture-in-Picture, parental controls, adaptive streaming, and more.
- **Smart Search & Filters**: Quickly find channels by name, language, country, or category.
- **Customizable**: Choose your theme, layout, and playback preferences.
- **No IPTV Service Provided**: Genova is a player only. You must provide your own IPTV sources.

## üöÄ Getting Started

1. **Download Genova IPTV** for your platform (coming soon to all major app stores and direct download).
2. **Add Your IPTV Source**:
   - Go to Settings > Streams
   - Enter your Xtream Codes credentials or M3U/M3U8 playlist URL
   - Genova will automatically fetch and organize your channels
3. **Browse & Watch**:
   - Use the Dashboard for what's live now
   - Explore categories, search, and mark favorites
   - Enjoy advanced playback features

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Does Genova provide IPTV channels?**
> No. Genova is a player only. You must provide your own legal IPTV sources (Xtream, M3U, etc.).

**Q: Is Genova free?**
> Yes! All core features are free. Premium unlocks advanced options and cloud sync.

**Q: Is my data private?**
> Yes. Your playlists and settings are stored locally unless you enable cloud sync (premium).

**Q: Where can I get help?**
> See the [User Documentation](https://docs.genova.app) or contact support below.
