FAQ

Questions & Answers

Does Genova provide IPTV channels?

No. Genova is a player only. You must bring your own legal IPTV source (Xtream Codes credentials or M3U/M3U8 playlist URL).

Is Genova free?

Yes, completely free. All features are available at no cost, no subscription, no ads.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your playlists and settings are stored locally on your device only. We don't collect or transmit any personal data.

I found a bug. Where do I report it?

Open an issue on GitHub Issues or post in our Google Group community.
