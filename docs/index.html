Roadmap & Beta Status

What we're working on

Genova is in active development. Here's a transparent look at known limitations, ongoing fixes, and upcoming features.

Strict Search Engine

Fixing

The global search currently requires exact word matches. "One Piece FR" won't find "One Piece". We're working on a more flexible search algorithm.

Video Player & PiP

Fixing

Media3 & VLC are being stabilized. Some audio codecs are missing and Picture-in-Picture (PiP) is currently disabled due to a regression.

Watch Progress Triggers

Fixing

The "Up Next" auto-play triggers exactly at the video's end instead of shortly before. Continue Watching saves too early. Both are being adjusted.

Adaptive UI Redesign

In Progress

We are completely redesigning the adaptive layouts for better platform-specific ergonomics (mobile vs. tablet vs. TV screens).

"For You" Dashboard

Planned

A new dedicated Home tab featuring personalized recommendations, newly added content, and your Continue Watching queue.

FAQ

Questions & Answers

Does Genova provide IPTV channels?

No. Genova is a player only. You must bring your own legal IPTV source (Xtream Codes credentials or M3U/M3U8 playlist URL).

Is Genova free?

Genova is completely free during the beta phase. A pricing model (such as a freemium plan) is being considered for when the application becomes stable in production.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your playlists and settings are stored locally on your device only. We don't collect or transmit any personal data.

I found a bug. Where do I report it?

Open an issue on GitHub Issues or post in our Google Group community.
