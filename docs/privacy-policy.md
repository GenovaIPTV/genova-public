# Privacy Policy – Genova IPTV

**Last Updated:** December 2024

---

## Introduction

Genova IPTV ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Genova IPTV application across all platforms (Android, iOS, tvOS, macOS, Windows, Linux, Android TV).

By using Genova IPTV, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **IPTV Source Information**: When you add IPTV sources (Xtream Codes credentials, M3U/M3U8 playlist URLs), this information is stored locally on your device.
- **Preferences & Settings**: Your app preferences, theme selections, playback settings, and custom playlists are stored locally.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Analytics**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (e.g., feature usage, crash reports, performance metrics).
- **Device Information**: Basic device information (platform, OS version, app version) may be collected for compatibility and support purposes.
- **Technical Logs**: Error logs and diagnostic information may be collected to identify and fix bugs.

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Streaming Content**: We do not collect, store, or monitor the content you stream through Genova IPTV.
- **IPTV Service Credentials**: Your IPTV service credentials are stored locally and encrypted on your device. They are never transmitted to our servers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **App Functionality**: To provide core features like playlist management, favorites, and playback controls.
- **Improvement & Support**: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve app performance.
- **Customer Support**: To respond to support requests and provide technical assistance.

---

## 3. Data Storage

All your data (IPTV sources, playlists, favorites, settings) is stored **locally on your device**. This means:
- Your data never leaves your device.
- You have full control over your data.
- No internet connection is required to access your locally stored information.

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

- **No Sale of Data**: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
- **Service Providers**: We may use trusted third-party services for analytics (anonymized usage data). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption**: Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
- **Local Encryption**: Sensitive data stored locally is encrypted.
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to user data, only for necessary operations.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data.
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics (where applicable).
- **Data Export**: Export your playlists and settings (when technically feasible).
- **Correction**: Correct inaccurate information.

To exercise these rights, contact us on our [Google Group](https://groups.google.com/g/genova-player).

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Genova IPTV is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Notifying you via the app (where applicable)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

---

## 9. Third-Party IPTV Services

**Important**: Genova IPTV is a player only. We do not provide IPTV services or content. When you use Genova with third-party IPTV sources:
- We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party IPTV providers.
- We do not monitor, store, or access the content you stream.
- Your use of third-party IPTV services is subject to their own terms and privacy policies.
- You are responsible for ensuring you have legal access to the content you stream.
